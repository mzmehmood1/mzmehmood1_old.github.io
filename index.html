Muhammad Zia Mehmood

I’m a PhD candidate at UC Berkeley, in the Business and Public Policy group at the Haas School of Business. I hold an MPP from Harvard's Kennedy School of Government, and a BSc in Economics from the Lahore University of Management Sciences (Pakistan).

I will be on the 2023/2024 academic job market.

My research interests are in development economics, political economy and behavioral economics.
